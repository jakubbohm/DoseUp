During the initial product-definition interview (2026-07-12), I declared the interview complete after four rounds and started writing the docs; Jakub stopped me — he had many more topics and notes to contribute.

**Why:** he wants explicit control over pacing and completeness. "I have everything I need" is his call to make, never mine. He said verbatim: "you should never decide yourself that you know everything - it's always up on me to decide if we should progress to the next stage."

**How to apply:** at the end of each stage or interview round, summarize the current state and ask whether to go deeper or advance; wait for an explicit go before producing stage outputs (docs, scaffolds, openspec changes). See also [[user-working-style]].
